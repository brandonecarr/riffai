FAQ

Common questions

Do I need an OpenAI account?
▾
Yes — RiffAI uses the OpenAI API to power your AI executives. You'll need an OpenAI API key, which you enter during setup. This means your conversations go directly to OpenAI from your machine, with no middleman. Typical usage costs a few dollars per month.
Is my business data private?
▾
Absolutely. RiffAI is a native macOS app that stores all your data locally. Your business context, standup history, and agent knowledge never leave your device (other than the API calls to OpenAI, which are governed by OpenAI's privacy policy).
How long does setup take?
▾
Under 5 minutes. The onboarding wizard walks you through your business profile, hires your AI executives, and briefs them on your company — all before your first standup.
Can I customize the AI executives?
▾
Yes. You can choose from preset C-suite roles (CGO, CMO, COO, CRO, CFO, CTO) with pre-built personas, or create fully custom agents with your own names, titles, and expertise areas.
Does it work on Windows?
▾
Yes! RiffAI is available as both a macOS app (.dmg) and a Windows installer (.exe). Both versions have full feature parity.
What if I want to change my team later?
▾
No problem. You can add, remove, or modify your AI executives at any time from the Settings panel. Changes take effect immediately on your next standup.
